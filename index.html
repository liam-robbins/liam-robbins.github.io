First Sortie

Febuary 4, 2018

Due to some mutual interests and acquaintances, I happened upon the opportunity to purchase an aging Capitani Romani class cruiser from an older gentlemen named Bob in Pasedena. The ship is a beauty, and she has seen quite alot of love over the years.

Bob belonged to a local hobby group named Southern California Region Attack Patrol (What a mouthful!). SCRAP runs RC ship battles on the first Saturday of every month at Prado Lake, and are part of a larger community collectively falling under the umbrella RC warship combat. Many in the group are old timers, but they were very welcoming to youngins like me.

I have since registered for membership, and have been placed as an associate captain on the SCRAP website. I attended the June 2018 along with a close friend of mine with my newly christened Pasta bote, only to be met by a...
